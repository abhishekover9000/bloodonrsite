Mission

“To build an upgraded streamlined healthcare system with the patient in mind" – BloodonR Team

Vision

At BloodonR we found massive problems in many of today’s blood bank centers, blood donation processes and the overall market, which lead us to creating and developing a new system for all hospitals and laboratories. One of those major problems was discovering that when you donate your blood at blood donation centers, events or mobile vehicles, they in turn sell your blood or plasma, ranging from $200-$2k per pint. Each donation is a pint or more! With this new system hospitals and laboratories can save millions of lives and save millions of dollars per year. With the added bonus of receiving compensation in the form of a gift for your effort and time.

Our Goal

Our goal is to start simple and small, show how we can turn one part of the health care system into a better system, while benefiting both sides of the fence. Once that is done, then we will rapidly expand into other sectors and departments of the health care system, positively effect change to ultimate get the system working properly and for the people once again.
